Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I get a Study Pass to unlock extra mock tests?
You can earn a Study Pass easily by clicking the "Earn Study Pass" button and watching a rewarded video ad inside the app. Each finished ad adds 1 Study Pass to your wallet.

2. Does the app require an internet connection?
Core study modes (Flashcards, Practice, and reviewing Wrong Answers) work offline. An internet connection is only required to load and watch rewarded ads to earn Study Passes.

3. Are the questions up-to-date?
Yes, all civics questions are based on the official United States Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) study guides.
